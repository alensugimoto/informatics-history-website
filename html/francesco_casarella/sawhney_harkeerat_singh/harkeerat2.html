Who is Bill Gates?
Microsoft is an American multinational technology company which develops and manufactures, licenses, supports, and sells computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and relates services. Microsoft is founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen on April 4, 1975. Bill Gates, in full William Henry Gates III was born in 28th October 1955. Since childhood Bill Gates has been interested in computers and has shown his interest but writing his first software program at the age of 13. In High-School itself he helped to form a group of programmers to computerized their school’s payroll system which later was known as Traf-O-Data. He later kept on adapting the different programming languages and later on made many software breakthroughs to not only found one of the most successful companies, but to actually make most widely used operating system around the world.
