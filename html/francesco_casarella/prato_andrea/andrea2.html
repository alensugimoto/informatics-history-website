Dirk Meyer
During his career Dirk Meyer worked as co-architect on two microprocessors for the Digital Equipment Corporation, then he worked in the microprocessors design team for Intel.
In 1995 he joined AMD as director of engineering for the first Athlon processor, on which the Athlon64x2 was based.
Meyer worked in many sectors of AMD, he was chief operating officer of AMD's Microprocessors Solutions Sector, then he worked also as chief operating officer of AMD, where he shared leadership and management of AMD with Hector Cruiz, the former CEO. In 2008 Dirk Meyer became the successor of Hector Cruiz as CEO of AMD.
