Interesting things about the Flash memory

Most expensive flash memory

The most expensive flash drive in the world was created by a Swiss jewellery company "La Maison Shawish". Its peculiarity is that this memory is covered with rubies, emeralds, rubies, diamonds and sapphire with a gold yellow and pink colour.

Flash memory in a fridge? and why?

The temperature can be the most important thing for the storage of flash memory because if it is adequate, the device can withstand up to 10 years! For example, a USB flash stored in a refrigerator has a longer storage life. At 100 °C, the USB flash will be completely damaged in 10 hours.

The invincible memory

USB flashes are very durable and can withstand extreme pressures and extreme temperature conditions. They can withstand large drops, shocks and are unlikely to suffer physical damage or wear and tear.
